Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why do I have to register?

    Registration is used to track user stats and progress atop the leaderboard. Your email is used as a unique identifier, and it is never used for any other purpose.



  • How was this app built?

    Questions were developed using a publically available Python dataset which contained over 50,000 lines from the script.



  • Can I play this app with my friends?

    Yes! There is a special "Challenge Friends" section that allows you to play with other people and receive the same questions in the same order! The fastest to finish with the most answers correct is the winner.



  • What if I want to use this for a bar trivia night?

    Dunder Mifflinaire is a great resource for a pub trivia night, whether that is picking out questions to use or using the app itself to keep track of answers!



  • Who do I contact if I have questions, comments, or concerns?

    Email dundermifflinaire@outlook.com!



  • I love this app! What can I do to help?

    The best way you can help is by rating the app on the App Store or Google Play!
